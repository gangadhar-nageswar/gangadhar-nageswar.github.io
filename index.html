Gangadhar Nageswar

I am a final-year masters student at Carnegie Mellon University, enrolled in the Robotic Systems Development (MRSD) program. I am currently working with Prof. Sebastian Scherer on the Wildfire Project. I have interned at the Milwaukee Tool Company as a Robotics Engineer during the summer'24. My current work specializes in 3D computer vision and SLAM, with applications in autonomous systems and manipulation. In addtion to this, I enjoy working on the systems engineering side of robotics as well.

Prior to this, I obtained my undergraduate degree in engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IITB). Here I conducted research under Prof. Mayank Baranwal on Physics-Informed Neural Networks for system identification for my undergraduate-thesis. During my third year, I was a research assistant at the A.R.M.S Lab, IIT Bombay where I worked on multi-agent patrolling using Q-Learning and Non-Linear Integer Programming.

Areas of Expertise: 3D Computer Vision, SLAM, Deep Learning, Systems Engineering

Experience
